w3connect is a secure private key vault designed for AI agents, preventing LLMs from directly accessing private keys while providing powerful blockchain interaction capabilities.

🔐

Private Key Isolation

Private keys are securely stored. AI can only perform authorized operations without direct access to sensitive keys.

⚡

Plug & Play AI

Seamlessly integrates with opencode, codex, claude, openclaw, nanobot and other major AI tools.

🛡️

Permission Control

Fine-grained permission management with configurable limits, expiration times, and operation types.

🔐

Google / Microsoft Auth

Two-factor authentication for transactions. Approve transfers via Google Auth or Microsoft Auth for enhanced security.

🔗

Multi-Chain Support

One-stop support for Base, Zentra, and other major blockchains.
